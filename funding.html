Empowering AI Agents with Crypto-Driven Funding

VC's? Not needed here. - We've got $Smarc

SmartArc is a B2B-focused AI automation agency revolutionizing funding with a Solana-based token. Our funding will be raised transparently and released gradually, ensuring stability and long-term growth.

Token Allocation

Circulating Supply (89%)

We’re launching fairly with 89% of tokens allocated to the community. It ensures the community is actively involved, widely represented, and genuinely empowered from the very beginning.

SmartArc Treasury for Partnerships (7%)

7% of tokens are allocated to our treasury, specifically set aside to reward participants, contributors, and innovators who help grow and enhance our ecosystem. These tokens will initially be locked for one month, with the unlocking schedule determined in collaboration with our community.

Team Wallet (4%)

4% of tokens are allocated for team operations and the continuous development of our AI agents. To ensure alignment with long-term goals, these tokens are locked and vested over 24 months.

SmartArc is dedicated to creating an open and inclusive ecosystem where everyone has the opportunity to contribute and be part of the journey. For full transparency, every wallet connected to the project will be publicly shared here. (Team & Treasury Wallets)

Token Utilities

adopt & save

Businesses integrating SmartArc AI agents receive a 35% discount when payments are made in $Smarc tokens. This incentivizes token utility and drives broader adoption within the ecosystem.

Buyback & Lock

10% of every integration fee will be used to buy $Smarc and locked for 12 months. This reduces the circulating supply and reinforces long-term stability while aligning ecosystem growth with business adoption.

join SmartArc & earn

Sales team members earn 5% of their sales value in $Smarc, aligning incentives, driving engagement, and fostering strong loyalty while encouraging consistent performance within the SmartArc ecosystem.
